San Andreas : Multipayer (SA:MP)

San Andreas Multiplayer is a Multiplayer Client for Grand Theft Auto : San Andreas, allows players to play with others on available servers with diffrent gamemodes such as FreeRoam, Cops & Robbers, TeamDeathmatch, Roleplay, etc. depending on the developers.

This Clients is not Rockstar official program, but rather the fanbase creation for them to be able to play together in the single player based game
